R packages from my PhD research
======

<img src="/images/spacrt-logo.png" align="left" width="120" style="margin-right: 1.2em; margin-bottom: 0.5em;"/>

**spacrt** — An R package implementing the saddlepoint approximation-based conditional randomization and independence tests for fast and accurate large-scale hypothesis testing. Companion software to Niu, Choudhury, and Katsevich (2024). ([GitHub](https://github.com/Katsevich-Lab/spacrt), [paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2407.08911v4), [code](https://github.com/Katsevich-Lab/spacrt-manuscript))

**perturbplanApp** — An interactive Shiny application for designing and planning single-cell CRISPR perturbation experiments, providing power and sample-size guidance for practitioners. ([GitHub](https://github.com/Katsevich-Lab/perturbplanApp))
